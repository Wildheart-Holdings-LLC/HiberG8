🔒 Traffic held. New page loads and downloads are paused until you Green Light traffic.

HiberG8 holds the browser's outbound traffic and pauses downloads until you review your connection and decide it's safe to resume. It cannot detect a VPN, so "require VPN" is a manual confirmation. This is a convenience layer on top of your VPN client's own kill-switch, not a replacement. It does not hibernate tabs, read page content, or send any data anywhere.

🕶 Incognito / InPrivate: HiberG8 does not run in private windows unless you opt it in. To use it there, open your browser's extension details and enable "Allow in Incognito" (Chrome) or "Allow in InPrivate" (Edge). This is your choice, not a default.
